About Me

I am a PhD student at the University of Michigan, working under Professor Neil Dasgupta. My research focuses on perovskite solar cells, photoluminescence metrology, and numerical methods for material analysis. I am particularly interested in developing low-cost, multiplexed depth-resolved PL systems to study carrier dynamics.
